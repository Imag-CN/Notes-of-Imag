>[!problem]
>Let $X$ and $Y$ be independent $n(0,1)$ random variables, and define a new random variable $Z$ by
>
>$$Z=\begin{cases}X&\text{if }XY>0\\-X&\text{if }XY<0.\end{cases}$$
>
>(a) Show that $Z$ has a normal distribution.
>
>(b) Show that the joint distribution of $Z$ and $Y$ is not bivariate normal.
