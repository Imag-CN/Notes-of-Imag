> [!note] Hilbert's Nullstellensatz (weaken)
> If $P(x,y)$ and $Q(x,y)$ are polynomials with complex coefficients then$$\{(x,y) \in \mathbb{C}^2 : P(x,y)=0\} = \{(x,y) \in \mathbb{C}^2 : Q(x,y)=0\}$$if and only if there exist positive integers $m$ and $n$ such that $P \mid Q^n$ and $Q \mid P^m$; or equivalently, if and only if $P$ and $Q$ have the same irreducible factors, possibly occurring with different multiplicities.

可以根据$C[x,y]$为UFD来解释, 但这只是希尔伯特零点定理的一个弱化版本.

该定理的完整版本有深刻的代数几何背景, 且在第三章证明Bezout定理时会使用, 这里给出表述.

>[!thereom] Hilbert's Nullstellensatz (Strong Form)
>Let $k$ be an algebraically closed field, $I \subseteq k[X_1, \ldots, X_n]$ be an ideal. Then:$$\mathbf{I}(\mathbf{V}(I)) = \sqrt{I}$$
>where:
>- $\mathbf{V}(I) = \{ \mathbf{a} \in k^n \mid f(\mathbf{a}) = 0 \, \forall f \in I \}$ (zero set of $I$)
>- $\mathbf{I}(V) = \{ f \in k[X_1, \ldots, X_n] \mid f|_V \equiv 0 \}$ (vanishing ideal of $V$)
>- $\sqrt{I} = \{ f \mid \exists m \geq 1 : f^m \in I \}$ (radical of $I$)


> [!notes] Theorem (Faltings)
> A complex algebraic curve of genus at least two has only finitely many points with rational coefficients.

该定理来源于Mordell的猜想.实际上可以推广到任意代数数域上(代数数域K上的亏格大于等于2的代数曲线至多有有限个K点).

n阶费马曲线($x^n+y^n=1$)没有奇点,所以亏格为$\dfrac{1}{2} (n-1)(n-2)$,因此其上至多有有限个有理点.(费马大定理表明没有非平凡的有理点.)

>[!theorem] 黎曼存在定理
>每个不可约, 光滑的射影复代数曲线, 都自然地是一个紧黎曼面.反之, 每个紧黎曼面都双全纯等价于某个不可约, 光滑的射影复代数曲线.

这个定理表明复代数曲线与紧黎曼面存在某种足够好的一一映射.
